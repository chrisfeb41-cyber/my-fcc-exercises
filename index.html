My Recipe: Avocado Egg Toast

Sometimes the best food is the simplest. You don't need a dozen of ingridients or hours in the kitchen to make something comforting and satisfying. That's the spirit of this 4-ingridient by 4-step recipe: the humble yet always delicious Avocado Egg Toast!

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • salt and pepper (to tsate)

Instructions

  1. Toast the bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork, season with salt and pepper
  3. Fry or poach the eggs to your liking
  4. Spread the avocado on toast, top with egg, sprinkle more pepper, and serve

Good for one serving. Served best with hot coffee or cold drinks.
